CORE FUNCTIONALITY
Cart System:

Store cart items in JavaScript array with localStorage persistence

Cart sidebar slides from right, shows all items with quantity controls (+/-) and remove button

Update cart badge count and total price in real time

"Proceed to Checkout" button opens order modal

Product Modal (Quick View):

Opens when clicking ANY product card

Shows: product title, price, main image, 5 thumbnail carousel (same image repeated), quantity selector (+/-)

Two buttons: "Add to Cart" (adds, closes modal, silent) and "Buy Now" (adds, closes modal, opens cart sidebar)

NO browser notifications/alerts when adding to cart

Order Modal (Checkout):

Form fields: First Name, Last Name, Address, WhatsApp Number, Notes (optional)

On submit: generates WhatsApp message with all cart items, totals, customer details

Sends to +923140607452, clears cart, closes modal, resets form

Message format includes product list with quantities, total price, customer info, "COD payment" note

Button Styling Rules (CRITICAL):

ALL "Add to Cart" and "Buy Now" buttons:

Text color: #8B4513 (wood color)

Border: 2px solid #8B4513

Background: transparent

On hover: only subtle background rgba(139, 69, 19, 0.08) appears, text stays wood color

"Proceed to Checkout" button: solid wood background (#8B4513) with white text

Category filter pills: border wood color, on active: solid wood background with white text
